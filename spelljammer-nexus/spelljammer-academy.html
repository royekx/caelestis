Overview

Spelljammer Academy is the Fleet's primary training institution for new spelljammer crews. Founded and funded by Mirt the Merciless, the Academy operates out of a fortified tower on the Sword Coast and accepts recruits from across Wildspace and beyond. Cadets are assessed, assigned crew roles, and eventually sent on live Fleet missions.

The Academy has a reputation for producing capable spacefaring crews. It also has a reputation for knowing more than it tells its cadets — which, given the recent string of burglaries, seems increasingly apt.

Security Levels

The Academy runs a five-tier access system. Each tier carries a colour and a rank. Cadets begin at Red. Moving up requires authorisation from command and the appropriate coloured security helmet.

Security checkpoints operate throughout the tower, staffed by veterans. Each guard carries a shortsword with a green-glowing blade. Any creature reduced to 0 hit points by one is instantly turned to dust. The warning posted throughout campus is not decoration.

Known Personnel
Mirt the Merciless — Headmaster. Founder. Known throughout Faerûn long before the Academy. His quarters are on an upper level — and apparently contain more than they should.
Boatswain Tarto — The crew's direct authority. Runs orientation, recalls cadets when free time is over, and communicates entirely in practical instructions. Quietly approving when it's earned.
Saerth Abyzine — Spelljammer instructor at the Nexus. Eerie, measured, and appears to notice more than she reacts to.
Sor'kur — Thri-kreen at Administration reception. Handles intake, welcome packs, and security vouchers with eight-limbed efficiency.
Mr. Blip — Quartermaster at Stores. Pragmatic. Helpful. Appears to know more about the supply ledgers — and their anomalies — than most.
Kip and Pik — Sky Dock crew. Efficient, almost mirrored in how they operate. They know which ships carry what.
