The Academy Thefts

What the Crew Knows
  • Burglaries have been occurring across Caelestis. Tarto confirmed this during orientation.
  • Navigational charts depicting Realmspace were stolen from the Spelljammer Nexus.
  • The thefts shook the Academy's investors enough to reduce monthly funding. Mirt is not happy.
  • Security checkpoints went into active operation as a result. The disintegration warning is being enforced.
  • The creature attack in Mirt's quarters during Voyage 001 was almost certainly a diversion — timed to pull attention while something else was taken.
  • Mirt and Mr. Blip connected the crate incident to the wider string of burglaries immediately after it was resolved.
  • Sol preserved one of the attacking creatures alive. The officers have a specimen to examine. What that yields is not yet known.
What Remains Unknown

Who is responsible. What is being taken beyond the navigational charts. Where the stolen materials are going. Whether the creatures in the crate were sourced locally or brought in from Wildspace. Whether the creature attack was planned specifically around the crew's errand — or whether the crew was incidental to a diversion targeting Mirt's quarters more broadly.

The officers know more than they have shared with cadets. That much is apparent. Whether that is standard protocol or something more deliberate is not yet clear.

Status

Ongoing as of Voyage 001. Investigation active at officer level. Cadet-level access to findings has not been granted.
