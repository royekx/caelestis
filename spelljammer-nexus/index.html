Wildspace Fundamentals
What every Spelljammer cadet learns in the first week. The shape of the universe, how ships move through it, and what lives out there.
Wildspace
The void between celestial bodies — planets, moons, asteroids, and stranger things. Ships sail it. Most things die in it. Cadets learn to love it anyway.
Crystal Spheres
Each world's solar system is enclosed within a vast crystalline shell. Realmspace is the sphere the crew currently operates in.
The Phlogiston
The chaotic, flammable flow between crystal spheres. Gods can't reach you here. That's either comforting or not, depending on the day.
Spelljammer Helms
A magical seat that lets a spellcaster pilot a ship through Wildspace. Speed, manoeuvrability, and endurance depend on the caster. The ship handles the rest.
Air Envelopes
Ships carry their own bubble of breathable air. Merging envelopes during boarding is how pirates get aboard. The simulation made that very clear.
Astral Reavers
The crew's first simulated enemy. Hostile humanoids encountered mid-void, fast and savage. The simulation called it training. It didn't feel like it.
