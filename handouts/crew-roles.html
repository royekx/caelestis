Your role adds to your normal actions — it doesn't replace them. You can still attack, cast spells, and use all your character abilities. Role abilities are additional options. Rank-ups are story milestones, not level gates — Academy graduation advances everyone to Sailor.
Spelljammer Pilot · Spellcaster required · One occupant only
Passive — Helm Bond The ship shares your initiative. You direct all ship actions on your turn — movement, turning, and weapon fire — without spending your own actions. You can sense everything on and around the vessel and target creatures you sense but cannot see.
Cadet (Starting Rank)
Move & Turn. Direct the ship to move up to its speed and turn up to its agility. No action required.
Onboard Defense. Action. A creature aboard makes a Wisdom save or takes 1d6 psychic damage and has disadvantage on its next attack roll or saving throw.
Sailor (Academy Graduation)
Expend Spell Slot. Action. Choose: Shields Up — ship gains +3 AC (+1 per slot level above 1st) until your next turn. Or Thrusters — +30 ft. speed (+15 per slot level above 1st) until your next turn.
Match Speed. Action. Mirror a target ship's movements, maintaining current distance. Ends if they move in a way your ship can't follow.
Officer (Field Proven)
Cast Through the Helm. Cast spells requiring line-of-sight but not line-of-effect. Casting time must be 1 action or less. Starting a new concentration spell ends helm concentration.
Jamming Charge. Bonus Action. Begin charging toward jamming speed. Takes 1 minute total. Pauses each turn you share space with another vessel.
Bridge (Senior Command)
Helm Surge. Action, 1/long rest. Ship speed doubles and it may turn twice this turn. You gain one level of exhaustion.
Extended Senses. Helm awareness extends 1 mile. Cannot be surprised while attuned. Advantage on initiative rolls when entering combat at the helm.

Captain Command · One occupant only
Passive — Command Presence Any bridge crew member may choose to delay their turn and act at your initiative instead of their own. You decide the order of those who do. Bridge crew who don't opt in act on their own initiative as normal.
Cadet (Starting Rank)
Fire at Will. Action. One Gunner of your choice may use their reaction to make one ship weapon attack.
Rally. Action. A crew member or officer within 30 ft. gains advantage on their next ability check, attack roll, or saving throw before your next turn.
Sailor (Academy Graduation)
Ship Grapple. Action. Ready the crew to throw grappling hooks. Next time your ship enters the same space as a target, make a Charisma (Water Vehicles) check contested by their Captain (DC 10 if none). On a success, both ships are grappled.
Full Speed Ahead. Action. The Spelljammer may use their reaction to immediately move the ship up to half its speed without turning.
Officer (Field Proven)
Boarding Party. Action (while ship-grappled). Willing bridge crew and eligible crew who haven't acted this turn can join. At the start of the next round, all board simultaneously.
Barrage. 1/short rest. When you use Fire at Will, choose a second Gunner to also use their reaction to attack.
Bridge (Senior Command)
Master Captain. Advantage on initiative rolls when entering combat. On the first round, bridge crew acting at your initiative may do so without penalty.
By My Command. 1/long rest. Give the entire bridge crew an additional reaction this round, usable only for bridge crew role actions.

Boatswain Ship Operations · Also called Bosun
Passive — Constant Upkeep At the start of your turn, the ship regains Bulwark Points equal to half its maximum (rounded up). This recovery is suppressed until you spend an action to extinguish it if the ship is on fire.
Cadet (Starting Rank)
Brace for Impact. Action. One 90° side of your ship has resistance to non-magical physical damage until the start of your next turn.
Emergency Repairs. Action. The ship gains 2d10 temporary hit points.
Sailor (Academy Graduation)
Work the Sails. Action. Furled (−500 ft. speed, +45° maneuverability, max 180°), Course-sail (standard), or Full-sail (+500 ft. speed, −45° maneuverability, min 0°).
Fix It Up. Action. DC 15 Intelligence (Carpenter's, Smith's, or Tinker's Tools) to repair a disabled weapon, module, or sail. On a success, it's restored to 1 hull point and functioning.
Officer (Field Proven)
Hurry It Up! Action, 1/minute. Bulwark restored to full. One Gunner of your choice may make one additional attack before the start of your next turn.
Hard Turn. Action, 1/short rest. The first time the ship turns this turn, its maneuverability increases by 90° (max 360°).
Bridge (Senior Command)
Expert Boatswain. Brace for Impact protects a 180° side instead of 90°.
Master Boatswain. Ship gains additional Bulwark Points equal to your proficiency bonus as a permanent bonus to its Bulwark maximum while you hold this role.

Gunner Artillery · Multiple occupants permitted
Passive — Weapons Familiarity Assigned to one ship weapon at the start of combat. Bonus action to switch to another weapon not currently operated by another Gunner — can be done between attacks.
Cadet (Starting Rank)
Open Fire. Action. d20 + proficiency + Str or Dex vs. AC. On a hit, roll weapon damage without adding your modifier.
Raw Instinct. The backfire score of any ship weapon you operate is reduced by 1.
Sailor (Academy Graduation)
Readied Attack. Action. Ready the Open Fire action. When your trigger is met, use your reaction to fire. Backfire score reduced by an additional 1.
Brace Shot. Once per turn when you hit, reduce the damage roll by your proficiency bonus to force the target ship 5 ft. in a direction of your choice (if possible).
Officer (Field Proven)
Trained Gunner. Class features that apply to weapon attacks (Extra Attack, Divine Smite, etc.) can apply to ship weapons. Check with your DM for edge cases.
Precise Shot. Once per turn when you hit with a ship weapon attack, add your ability score modifier to the damage roll.
Bridge (Senior Command)
Distracting Shot. 1/short rest. When you hit, add your proficiency bonus to damage. The next attack against that target before your next turn has advantage.
Master Gunner. Precise Shot applies to all your ship weapon attacks. Crew requirement of weapons you operate reduced by 1 (min 1). On a hit you may choose to disable a specific component once its hull points reach 0.

Navigator Coordination & Hazards · Multiple occupants permitted
Passive — Situational Awareness +2 to all hazard group checks you personally contribute to. Once per combat at the start of your first turn, call out a tactical read — enemy heading, weapon count, nearest cover — granting one ally advantage on their first action this combat.
Cadet (Starting Rank)
Coordinate Movement. Action. A friendly creature within 60 ft. immediately moves up to half its speed. Doesn't provoke opportunity attacks.
Take Aim. Action. A weapon crew within 10 ft. gains advantage on its next attack roll within normal range before the end of your next turn.
Sailor (Academy Graduation)
Covering Maneuver. Action. A friendly creature within 60 ft. — the next attack roll made against that creature before your next turn has disadvantage.
Plot the Course. Once per hazard encounter, call for one failed hazard check to be rerolled. The second result stands.
Officer (Field Proven)
Coordinate Attacks. Action. A friendly creature within 60 ft. immediately makes one melee attack, ranged attack, or casts a spell with a casting time of 1 action or bonus action.
Take the Initiative. Action. Swap initiative positions with a willing creature within 60 ft. If they haven't acted this round, they take their turn immediately after yours. You don't take another turn until the next round.
Bridge (Senior Command)
Anticipate Hazard. When entering a hazard encounter, you and all allies who can hear you have advantage on the first check they make.
Tactical Mastery. You may use Coordinate Movement as a bonus action instead of an action. You may use both Coordinate Movement and Coordinate Attacks in the same turn.

Medical Officer Triage & Recovery · Multiple occupants permitted
Passive — Constant Vigilance Any creature making a death saving throw within 30 ft. of you adds 1d4 to the roll.
Cadet (Starting Rank)
Tend to Officer Injuries. Action. Creature within 5 ft. at 0 HP becomes stable. Otherwise gains temporary HP equal to 1d6 + your Wisdom modifier.
Neutralize Poison. Action. End the poisoned condition on one creature within 5 ft.
Sailor (Academy Graduation)
Tend to Crew Injuries. Action. Roll a d4; that many crew members at 0 HP regain consciousness with 4 HP.
Field Triage. Action. A creature within 5 ft. suffering exhaustion loses one level.
Officer (Field Proven)
Steady the Crew. Action. All crew and officers who can hear you and are frightened or charmed may immediately end that condition.
Improved Treatment. Tend to Officer Injuries now restores 2d8 + Wisdom modifier temporary HP and can end one condition: poisoned, frightened, or blinded.
Bridge (Senior Command)
Crisis Response. Reaction, 1/long rest. When an officer within 60 ft. drops to 0 HP, move up to your speed toward them and stabilize them or restore them to 1 HP.
Combat Ready. At the start of combat, grant temporary HP equal to your Wisdom modifier to each officer within 30 ft. as a free action.
