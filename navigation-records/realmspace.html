Realmspace
Crystal Sphere

Realmspace is the crystal sphere that contains Toril and its sister worlds — the origin point for most of the crew and the sphere where Caelestis Academy operates. It is one of the most thoroughly charted spheres accessible from the academy's position, and for most cadets it represents the boundary between the known and everything else.

The sphere is organized around a central sun, with its worlds divided into the close-orbiting Inner Worlds and the distant Outer Worlds. Travel times are measured in days from the sun, a standard the academy uses for routing and provisioning. Anadia, nearest the sun, is barely half a day out; H'Catha, the outermost charted world, sits sixteen days from the center at the edge of the sphere — the last landmark before the Flow carries vessels into the Deep Astral and, eventually, toward other spheres entirely.
