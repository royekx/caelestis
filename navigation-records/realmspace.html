Realmspace
Crystal Sphere

Realmspace is the crystal sphere that contains Toril and its sister worlds — the origin point for most of the crew and the sphere where Caelestis Academy operates. It is one of the most thoroughly charted spheres accessible from the academy's position, and for most cadets it represents the boundary between the known and everything else.

The sphere's Flow access points are well-documented. Its worlds range from the densely populated and politically complex to the strange and barely habitable. H'Catha, the outermost world, sits at the edge of the sphere — the last landmark before the Flow carries vessels into deeper wildspace and eventually to other spheres entirely.
