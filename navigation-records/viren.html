Viren System
Uncharted — Beyond Realmspace

Viren is known by name and by implication. No official navigation record exists for it in the academy's accessible charts. What is known comes from a single source: Mirt the Merciless, who owns a magical wardrobe that opens onto an extradimensional space tied to a location within the Viren system. The wardrobe was gifted to him by a spellcaster named Zerathis.

One member of the crew has heard a voice from inside that wardrobe. The voice said only: go there.
