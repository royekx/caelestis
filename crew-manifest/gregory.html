Gregory
Autognome · Cadet

Bronze-skinned with small round glasses and a neat mustache, Gregory has the quality of someone who always measures the room before stepping into it. He is thoughtful to the point of hesitation, precise to the point of self-correction, and dutiful in a way that reads less like rule-following and more like genuine belief in order.

He is farther from home and his maker than he has ever been, and something changed the day he moved into his bunk. A pull in his core — directional, purposeful, toward somewhere he cannot yet name. He has told no one.
