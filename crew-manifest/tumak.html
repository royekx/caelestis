Tumak
Human · Spelljammer Academy

Slight of build, swallowed by an oversized coat that seems to belong to someone larger. Tumak mutters reassurances to himself in the spaces between actions, as though keeping steady requires constant negotiation. He is tougher than he looks — takes hits that should drop him and stays standing.

He is searching for family. He voiced it plainly, unprompted, during the walk to Mirt's quarters. Boogie told him simply: now they have a crew, and the crew will help him find them.
