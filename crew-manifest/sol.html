Sol
Human · Cadet

Tall, pale-skinned, dark-haired, with eyes the colour of blank white sky. Sol has the look of someone who has survived things by learning to read them before they happen. He moves deliberately. He speaks when it matters.

When asked why he came to Caelestis, he answered simply: his life had always turned in unlucky directions, and he wanted to spend it exploring anyway. There is no bitterness in the statement. It reads as fact — a decision made clearly and held to.
