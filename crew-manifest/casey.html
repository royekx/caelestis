Casey
Hadozee · Spelljammer Academy

Casey moves through rooms the way light moves through water — present but hard to pin down. Barefoot. A pale green cloak stitched with constellations that don't belong to any charted sky. He answers direct questions sideways, and sideways questions not at all.

In the Spelljammer Nexus he struggled until he stopped trying to force the helm and found a posture that felt like his own. Whatever that posture is, it is entirely his. He has a direction now — a place he heard a voice tell him to go. He has not told anyone what it is.
