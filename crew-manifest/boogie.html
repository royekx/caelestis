Boogie
Plasmoid · Spelljammer Academy

Boogie arrived at Caelestis the way he has arrived everywhere — in motion. Semi-transparent and deep blue, he carries a small glittering stone somewhere inside himself and a shield that rarely leaves his arm. His past is one of borrowed identities and odd jobs, drifting between spaces that were never quite his own. He has no family he can name. He has had no lasting home.

What Caelestis offered him, and what no place before it had, was a reason to stay. During spelljamming training he found something he hadn't expected: a purpose that fit the shape of him. He doesn't want the helm. He wants to keep the ship alive — to make sure it carries others safely through whatever comes. When he said it aloud, something inside him answered back.
