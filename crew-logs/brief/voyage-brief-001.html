The Shape of a Crew
Voyage 001 Brief → Detailed Log
First day of orientation at Spelljammer Academy — simulation exercise, cadet quarters assignment, sky dock tour, spelljammer helm training, gymnasium assault course. The day ends with a delivery errand to Mirt's quarters that turns into a creature attack: a coordinated diversion run while thieves hit multiple locations across the academy at the same time.

The Simulation
  • Cadets woke on the deck of the Moonraider mid-battle, no warning — Captain Sardax bleeding, reavers over the rail
  • Gregory hid and survived; Tumak took heavy fire but held; Sol planted himself at a ballista and killed an attacker; Casey fought and went defensive with cold magic; Boogie moved toward Tumak to help
  • Boatswain Tarto froze the illusion and revealed it: the memory of Captain Sardax's real last battle. A test with no winning condition. Courage mattered. So did knowing when to retreat.
  • Tarto called out each cadet's best moment — Casey's spellwork, Gregory's quick thinking, Sol's battlefield control, Tumak's toughness, Boogie's instinct to help over glory
Orientation
  • Administration: Met Sorcur (thri-kreen, telepathic). Received packets, vouchers, temporary access stars. Caelestis is a machine of access and hierarchy.
  • Cadet Quarters: Another cadet, Vena, tried to take Boogie's corner bunk. Boogie held his ground; Sol moved close to back him. Vena backed off. First moment of the crew taking each other's side.
  • Sky Dock: Tour with Kip and Pik. Learned helm basics: ship and spellcaster become one motion. Boogie spotted a suspicious exchange between a dock officer and a merchant.
  • Quartermaster (Mr. Blip): Weapons and gear issued. Gregory visibly unsettled by Mr. Blip — an autognome who looks too much like him. Sol talked his way into heavier arms. Boogie charmed a light hammer. Thinking about repair, not command.
Spelljammer Nexus
  • Arrived during an ectoplasmic breach elsewhere in the tower
  • Saerth Abyzine ran the helm exercise: guide a miniature ship over a floating field using mind and magic
  • Boogie took to it immediately. Casey struggled until he stopped forcing it. Gregory overcame thinking to the destination before the method. Sol navigated by anticipating danger. Tumak managed — Saerth noted a divided quality in him.
  • Afterward, Boogie said it aloud: he doesn't want the helm. He wants to keep the ship alive.
Gymnasium
  • Assault course: false explosions, electrified water, swinging blades, final bell climb
  • Gregory saw through the first illusion and called it to Sol. Sol and Tumak paid in pain but refused to stay down. Casey crossed with ease.
  • Sol fell hard enough to be dragged aside; forced himself back after Boogie shouted him onward. Boogie used magic to help Gregory on the final ascent.
  • Boatswain's assessment: "The lag between you is good. You're starting to move as a crew."
The Mirt Errand
  • Tasked to deliver a crate to Mirt the Merciless in bridge quarters
  • En route: Boogie described a life of drifting, loneliness, no family. Told Tumak (who is searching for his own): "Now we have a crew. The crew will help you find them."
  • Mirt's room: Hungover, cluttered, wardrobe opening onto Astral night. Crate released worm-like centipede creatures — fight broke out.
  • Sol subdued the last creature alive rather than kill it. Mirt praised the call. Rewarded the group with gold.
  • Mirt told Casey privately: the wardrobe is a replica of a place beyond Hakatha in the Viren star system, gifted by a spellcaster named Zerathis.
  • Mr. Blip arrived with officers: the attack on Mirt was a diversion. Multiple burglaries across the academy — the cadets passed through the centre of it.
End of Day
  • Casey slipped into the wardrobe, saw unfamiliar constellations, heard a voice say "Go there." Grabbed a plain shirt and scratched the star pattern onto it as a quick reference. Passed it to Tumak, who hid it without question.
  • Gregory felt a new pull in his core — directional, toward somewhere unknown.
  • Sol: "My life always turned unlucky. I wanted to spend it exploring anyway."
  • Boogie reached into his chest and pulled out a tiny model spelljamming ship — same kind from training. Half of it was missing.
