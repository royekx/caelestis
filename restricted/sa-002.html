Above Bridge — Historical Reference. This registry records unusual celestial phenomena observed across surveyed spheres. Entries are filed for record only and do not constitute determination of cause. Subsequent anomalous activity, where recorded, is held under separate cover.
Summary

Fleet Intelligence maintains a standing registry of unusual celestial phenomena reported by Fleet vessels, surveyed parties, and contracted observers across surveyed spheres. The majority of entries describe ordinary astronomical events recovered without anomaly. A smaller subset have been flagged for follow-up that has not materialised. The selected entries below are reproduced for record. Detail entries, where they exist, are held under separate cover and not included in this distribution.

Notation

Status reflects the disposition of the corresponding follow-up filing. Closed entries have no outstanding observation requirements. Monitoring entries are referred to ongoing sector or sphere observation. Open entries indicate phenomena that have not been satisfactorily accounted for. None of the entries in this distribution include detail on subsequent anomalous activity, if any was recorded.
