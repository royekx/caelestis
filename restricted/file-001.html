Summary

Following the theft of navigational charts from the Spelljammer Nexus during Cycle 1492, Fleet Intelligence conducted a cross-institutional review of similar incidents over the prior eighteen months. The review identified a pattern of coordinated theft across four Fleet installations, including Caelestis.

The incidents are not isolated acts of opportunism. They constitute a systematic acquisition campaign conducted by one or more external parties with precise knowledge of Fleet scheduling, security rotations, and internal layout. This level of operational detail implies either significant prior surveillance or an internal source.

Affected Installations

Confirmed theft incidents sharing the acquisition pattern:

· Caelestis (Spelljammer Academy) — Navigational charts, Nexus library. Cycle 1492.
·              Fleet Relay Station — Navigational charts, route ledgers. Cycle 1491.
·              — Survey vessel log archive. Cycle 1491.
·                  — Transit manifest records. Cycle 1490.

Pattern Analysis

All stolen materials share a common navigational scope: routes in the outer Realmspace band and immediate extra-sphere transit corridors. No incident targeted operational materials, personnel records, or financial assets. The selection is precise — whoever directs this operation knows exactly which charts they need and where they are stored.

Taken together, the recovered materials would provide a complete navigation picture of routes to a location                  not represented in any Fleet registry. This destination is the assessed objective of the acquisition campaign.

Operational Signature

No operative has been apprehended. Two incidents produced physical evidence pointing to a shared logistics chain: a small pressed wax disc bearing an unidentified symbol was recovered at the              installation and again at Caelestis during processing of the Mirt incident. Symbol analysis is pending. The mark has not been matched to any known registered organisation.

Assessment

The acquisition campaign is ongoing. The directing party still requires charts from      additional sources before a complete navigation picture can be assembled. The window to act remains open — but it is closing.
