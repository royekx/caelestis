Constellation Shirt
Clothing · Unregistered

A plain shirt with a star pattern scratched onto the fabric by hand. Nothing remarkable about the object itself — the significance is entirely in what the drawing depicts. Casey made it on the spot after entering Mirt's wardrobe, using the shirt as an improvised sketchpad to capture the constellation arrangement he saw before he forgot it. A rough reference, the kind you scratch out when you have no paper.

Casey took it from inside the wardrobe. He passed it to Tumak without explanation shortly after. Tumak hid it behind the curtains of his bunk. Neither action was observed by Mirt or academy staff.

Voyage 001
Casey took a plain shirt on his way out of Mirt's wardrobe during Voyage 001 and scratched the star pattern he had seen onto the fabric as an on-the-spot reference sketch.
The drawing depicts a star arrangement that does not correspond to any known Realmspace constellation. Casey believes it maps to the Viren system.
Transferred to Tumak's keeping without explanation. Currently hidden in his bunk.
