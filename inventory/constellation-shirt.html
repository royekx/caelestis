Constellation Shirt
Clothing · Unregistered

An ordinary-looking shirt, except for the markings — a star pattern stitched or burned into the fabric that does not correspond to any constellation in Realmspace's night sky. The pattern was recognised by one crew member as matching what they saw when they entered Mirt's wardrobe.

Casey took it from inside the wardrobe. He passed it to Tumak without explanation shortly after. Tumak hid it behind the curtains of his bunk. Neither action was observed by Mirt or academy staff.
