Crimson Bag of Holding
Magical Item · Unregistered

A deep crimson bag, soft to the touch, that holds considerably more than its exterior suggests. Standard bag of holding construction, but the colouration and quality of the material suggest it was made to order rather than sourced from a general supplier.

It was found inside the extradimensional space of Mirt's private wardrobe during the crate delivery incident. It was not on any manifest. Its presence there — alongside other valuables — was either coincidence or deliberate placement. It has not been declared to academy command.
