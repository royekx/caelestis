Saerth Abyzine
Unknown · Spelljammer Nexus Instructor

Saerth Abyzine runs the Spelljammer Nexus exercises with the particular patience of someone who has watched a great many people fail to understand the helm before finally understanding it. He does not explain so much as observe — watching each student's approach, waiting for the moment they stop fighting the process, and naming what went wrong clearly when the time is right.

He ran the crew's first helm training: guiding miniature spelljamming ships across a floating field using mind and magic. His assessments were direct and individual. He noticed what each cadet brought to the exercise and, more usefully, what each one was getting in their own way.
