Kip & Pik
Gnomes · Sky Dock Instructors

Kip and Pik function so closely in tandem that distinguishing one from the other mid-conversation is more effort than it is worth. They finish each other's sentences, anticipate each other's points, and occasionally seem to arrive at the same conclusion from opposite directions at exactly the same moment. Whether this is a product of long partnership or something more is not recorded.

They guided the crew through the Sky Dock on orientation day, walking them through the anatomy of a spelljamming vessel and the fundamental principle of the helm: that ship and spellcaster, once joined, become a single motion.
