Mirt the Merciless
Human · Academy Head

Mirt the Merciless is the head and founder of Caelestis — a man whose title suggests ruthlessness but whose quarters suggest a deep and practiced comfort with excess. His chambers are cluttered with the residue of status: fine clothes in piles, curiosities on every surface, a fishbowl containing two octopi named Flotsam and Jetsam. He was half-buried in a hangover when the cadets arrived.

Beneath the mess there is a sharp mind and a well-calibrated instinct for who matters. He praised Sol's decision to keep the creature alive without being prompted. He handed the cadets real gold. And he told Casey — in private — more than he perhaps intended to.

His quarters were the site of a coordinated creature attack that served as a diversion for wider academy burglaries.
