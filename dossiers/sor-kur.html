Sor'kur
Thri-kreen · Academy Administration

Sor'kur occupies the administration desk with the quiet efficiency of someone who has processed more incoming cadets than she could count. She does not speak — she transmits. Her voice enters the mind directly, precise and unhurried, bypassing the need for ears entirely. Whether this is considered unusual by the standard of her post is unclear. Nobody appears to have complained.

She issued the crew their orientation packets, access vouchers, and temporary clearance stars without ceremony. She is the first face of Caelestis's bureaucratic architecture — and the architecture, as the crew would learn, runs deep.
