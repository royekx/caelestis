Boatswain Tarto
Hadozee · Training Officer

The scars Boatswain Tarto carries are a record, not a decoration. One of her wing flaps was torn away long ago. She remains as capable as she ever was. She is never one to waste words, and she extends that policy equally to cadets and senior officers alike.

She knows what she is doing with new recruits. The simulation she put the crew through was the doomed final battle of Captain Sardax — a test with no winning condition. The lesson was deliberate: courage matters, but so does knowing when to retreat. She told them as much, and meant it.
